Popular Movies app
(Android)

A movie suggestion app created as part of the Android Developer Nanodegree program. Powered by The Movie DB.

Features:

  • Sort movies by popularity or by ratings
  • Search function to find any movie in the database
  • See and share trailers and images, read reviews
  • Material Design
  • Tablet UI

    • Stock Hawk app
    (Android)

    An app to track prices of stocks over time. Developed as part of the Android Developer Nanodegree program.

    Features:

  • Add stocks via symbol
  • Subscribe to your stocks
  • See detailed information about stocks
  • Graph of price history over 1 month/6 months/1 year
  • Homescreen widget to keep you up-to-date

    • Notes app
    (Android)

    A simple note-taking app for Android that is fully open-source. Developed as a fun side-project in a period of 2 days during the summer vacations.

    Features:

  • Write notes with Title, Description, and Content
  • Archive notes
  • Share your notes
  • Create notifications from your notes
  • Material Design

    • Pacman clone
    (C language)

    A simple clone of the classic arcade game by Toru Iwatani. Developed as First Year Computer Engineering project for Stuctured Programming Approach course.

    Features:

  • Play as Pacman and eat all the pellets
  • Avoid the three ghosts
  • Three different difficulty levels (plus a hidden level)
